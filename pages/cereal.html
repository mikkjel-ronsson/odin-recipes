How To Cereal

Ingredients

  • Milk - however much you want of whichever kind you want.
  • Cereal - again - whatever you want. IDGAF, bro.

Instructions

  1. Get a bowl. It can be clean, dirty, new, whatever. Just get something to pour things into
  2. Pour cereal in to bowl. Feed yourself.
  3. Add milk. Some like it soggy, some like it with crunch. Figure out what you like best
  4. When the food is gone, drink the remaining milk like it's the soul of your vanquished foe. WASTE NOTHING.
  5. Leave empty bowl in your sink to clean later, when, you know, there's more dishes or something
